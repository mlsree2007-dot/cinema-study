# 🎬 Cinema Study

**Cinema Study** is a high-discipline productivity timer that replaces the traditional Pomodoro technique with the immersive runtimes of Indian Cinema. 



## 🚀 Live Demo
**[View the App Live Here](https://lathikkasree.github.io/cinema-study/)**

---

## 💡 The Concept
Traditional study timers (25 mins) often break the "Flow State" required for complex engineering tasks or deep problem-solving. This app leverages the 2.5 to 3-hour duration of movies to encourage long-form focus.

### The "Theater Rules":
1. **No Pausing:** Once the session starts, the clock cannot be stopped.
2. **Mandatory Intermission:** A 20-minute break is automatically triggered at the halfway point.
3. **Task-Oriented:** You don't just "watch" a movie; you complete a specific study task during its duration.

---

## 🛠️ Features
- **Dual Input System:** Choose from a curated list of Indian blockbusters (Leo, Vikram, RRR, etc.) or enter a custom movie and duration.
- **Dynamic Timer:** Tracks hours, minutes, and seconds with a cinematic UI.
- **Intermission Overlay:** Automatically manages your break time so you don't have to.
- **Session History:** Saves your completed "Blockbusters" to your browser's local storage.
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for both Desktop and Mobile use.

---

## 💻 Tech Stack
- **HTML5 & CSS3:** Custom dark-themed UI with cinema aesthetics.
- **Vanilla JavaScript:** Core logic for timer arrays, intervals, and state management.
- **Local Storage API:** For persistent data tracking without a backend.

---

## 📖 How to Use
1. **Select a Movie:** Use the dropdown or type in your own movie and its runtime.
2. **Enter your Task:** Type exactly what you plan to study (e.g., *Mechanical Vibrations Chapter 2*).
3. **Start the Show:** Hit "Start Session" and begin your deep work.
4. **Take the Break:** When the Intermission screen appears, step away from your desk for 20 minutes.
5. **Finish Strong:** Complete the second half to add the session to your history!
